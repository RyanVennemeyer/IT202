Interesting facts about Sian Kaʼan
  • Established as a UNESCO: 1986
  • In the year 2000, 36,000 annual visitors were recorded
  • Area: 1,080 mi²
  • UNESCO Site Id: 410
  • Over 850 vascular plants grow in the park, including 120 species of wooded trees.
  • Tropical forests and mangrove marshes comprise most of the vast expanse, housing 400 species of fish and 300 types of birds. Exotic cats like jaguars, pumas, and ocelots roam through the jungle, although it’s unlikely you’ll spot these elusive beasts on a tour.
